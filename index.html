About KeyForge

What We Do

KeyForge is a professional license key generation platform designed for software developers, vendors, and businesses that need reliable, secure, and scalable licensing solutions.

Why Choose Us

Our system generates unique, random, and cryptographically secure keys. Each key is independently generated with maximum entropy to ensure uniqueness and security across all your licensing needs.

Key Format

Every license key is 32 characters long, containing uppercase letters (A-Z) and numbers (0-9). This format ensures compatibility with most licensing systems and maximum customization potential.

Security Focus

Your key generation happens entirely in your browser. No data is sent to servers. Complete privacy and security with open-source algorithms and transparent operations.
